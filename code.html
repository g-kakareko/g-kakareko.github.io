Code

You can find my code on github . Most of the repositories, especially the one used for research are private. If you find some of my work interesting, feel free to email me , I will be happy to share some of the private repositories.

In 2017, together with my college Daniel Bis during the programing competition, we created the online application Strom-Risk designed to estimate and mitigate the house damage. The application is online, and you can access the app here here . Strom-Risk is a web application design to calculate the potential house loss during the hurricane. The Strom-Risk was inspired by the previous hurricane seasons. We hope that, such an easy online tool will help homeowners in mitigation decision making, which will decrease the future hurricane loss. Based on the best of my knowledge, storm-risk is the only free tool for loss calculation available for homeowners. The science behind the application is consistent with the current state of art and is based on my publications.
