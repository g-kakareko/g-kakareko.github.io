About

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering and MS. Student in Computer Science at Florida State University. In my current research I try to combine the knowledge from both fields. My research area includes topics like: multi scale loss estimation, risk analysis, machine learning, image recognition, mechanics of structures. For the last two years I have been working as a data scientist at Risk Management Solutions where I helped to develop the HWind products. Before, I worked at Warsaw University of Technology WUT at the Department of Structural Mechanics and Computer Aided Engineering, where I was an instructor of the Computer Engineering Graphics class. My research at WUT focused on assessing effective moduli of planar composites as well as computing effective membrane and bending stiffness.

Now, some less boring facts about me. I was a professional runner, Polish National Championship multi-medalist, member of Polish Athletic National Team and, to this day, Polish National Record Holder (400 relay, under 20). My passion is scuba diving. I am an advanced open water scuba diver.
